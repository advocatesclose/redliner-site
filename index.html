Redliner

A Mac OS app developed to support critical law firm workflows through version control, redlining, and machine learning[cite: 3, 4, 5].

The app is approved and available for sale on the Apple App Store[cite: 6].

Core Capabilities

  • Automated Tax Assets: Produce hardware-bound machine learning datasets without extra work[cite: 74].
  • On-Device Security: Processing happens entirely on your machine, not the cloud[cite: 41].
  • Performance: Compare 300-page documents in under one-tenth of a second[cite: 43].
